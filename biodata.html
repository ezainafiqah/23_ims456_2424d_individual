Hello everyone,

I am Ezain Afiqah, a 22-year-old from Malaysia. Embracing the beautiful teachings of Islam, I find solace and strength in my religion. As a Malay, I take great pride in my heritage and the rich cultural traditions that come with it.

Throughout my life, I have been drawn to the world of martial arts. As a previous student of Silat Cekak Pusaka Hanafi, Silat Gayong, and Taekwondo, I have not only honed my physical abilities but also embraced the values of discipline and perseverance that these martial arts instill.

Beyond the thrill of combat, I am also a nature enthusiast. Exploring the great outdoors, surrounded by lush greenery, is a source of inspiration and peace for me. Flowers, in particular, hold a special place in my heart, and their beauty never fails to bring joy to my soul.

Sports play a significant role in my life, and I am proud to call myself a badminton athlete. The adrenaline rush on the court and the camaraderie with fellow players are experiences that drive me to push my limits and continuously improve.

In the spiritual realm, I recently embarked on a meaningful journey, having completed my Umrah last February. This pilgrimage has deepened my connection with my faith and allowed me to find a sense of purpose and meaning in life.

As an individual, I am always eager to learn, grow, and embrace new experiences. Life's adventures are vast, and I am excited to navigate through its twists and turns, seeking knowledge and spreading kindness along the way.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. I look forward to engaging in interesting conversations and learning from each of you. Let's embark on this journey together, exploring the wonders of life and the world around us.
